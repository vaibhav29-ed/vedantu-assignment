Listening History

Spotify uses your listening history to create personalised recommendations, discover weekly playlists, and show you what's trending.

If you turn this off:

  • Made For You playlists will stop updating
  • Your home screen will show generic charts
  • You can still search and play any music
💻

Device & Network Info

We collect device type, OS version, and network data to optimise streaming quality and fix technical issues.

If you turn this off:

  • Audio quality may default to standard
  • App performance may be less optimised
  • Core playback remains unaffected
📍

Location

Your approximate location helps us show local charts, events near you, and region-relevant content.

If you turn this off:

  • Local charts and events won't be available
  • Region-specific content won't appear
  • Core playback remains unaffected
🔗

Third-Party Sharing

We share limited data with partners for targeted advertising (Free tier) and analytics. We never sell your data.

If you turn this off:

  • Ads will be less relevant (Free tier)
  • No analytics data shared with partners
  • Core playback remains unaffected
